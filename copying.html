Story & scope

In mid 2014 The Modern House partnered with PRÆSENS and London-based FIELD PROJECTS in the strategic redesign of the company’s digital properties. Together we identified a distinct disconnect between The Modern House digital presence and the business’s true voice and æsthetic ethos.

Solution

Focused on breaking the boundaries of traditional online real estate and more closely adhering to the company’s modernist influences, we created a visually striking, gracefully responsive platform that streamlines and improves business tools, more powerfully roots the brand’s essence and visual identity, and provides superior mobile experience. An added editorial blog creates valuable dialogue with the community, offering an opportunity to further define the company’s personality.
